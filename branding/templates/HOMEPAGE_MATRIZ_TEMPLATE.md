## üìù Content Writing Guidelines

### Tone Distribution Per Section

**Hero/Opening** (First 500 words):
- Higher poetic percentage (engage emotionally)
- Clear user-friendly value prop
- Credible academic grounding

**Main Content** (Middle 1,500 words):
- Follow exact domain tone distribution
- Vary vocabulary families every 200-300 words
- Include technical specs where appropriate

**Call-to-Action** (Final 500 words):
- Higher user-friendly percentage
- Clear, actionable language
- Inspiring poetic close
