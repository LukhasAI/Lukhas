## üìä Usage Guidelines

### Selection Criteria
- **Short projects**: Use Generic Stance (A) or Vocabulary Preamble (C)
- **Brand materials**: Use Longer Brand Header (B)
- **Technical docs**: Use Technical Header (D) or Development Philosophy (E)
- **Creative work**: Use Creative Process Header (F)
- **Trust/security**: Use Security Statement (G)
- **User-facing**: Use UX Philosophy (H)

### Customization Tips
- **Replace "system"** with specific product names when appropriate
- **Add domain-specific terms** while maintaining the stance and rhythm
- **Combine snippets** for longer introductions (maintain 40/40/20 balance)
- **Test readability** with the "grandparent test" (40% plain clarity)
