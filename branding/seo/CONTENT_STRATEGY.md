```
Pillar Page (2000-3000 words)
```

## Internal Linking Strategy

### Principles

1. **Pillar-Cluster Linking**: Every cluster article links back to its pillar
2. **Cross-Cluster Linking**: Related clusters link to each other (semantic relevance)
3. **Evidence Linking**: Technical claims link to evidence pages
4. **Strategic Depth**: 3-5 internal links per article
5. **Contextual Anchors**: Natural, keyword-rich anchor text

### Link Equity Distribution

```
Homepage
    ├── Pillar 1 (lukhas.ai) - 10% equity
    ├── Pillar 2 (lukhas.dev) - 10% equity
    ├── Pillar 3 (lukhas.com) - 30% equity (highest commercial value)
    ├── Pillar 4 (lukhas.eu) - 15% equity
    └── Pillar 5 (lukhas.app) - 35% equity (highest search volume)

Each Pillar
    ├── Cluster 1 (equal distribution)
    ├── Cluster 2
    └── ... (10-15 clusters)
```

### No Orphan Pages

**Rule**: Every page must be linked from at least 2 other pages.

**Validation**: Run `tools/optimize_internal_links.py --check-orphans`
