# LUKHAS.EU Brand Guide
## European Ethics & Compliance Portal

---

## Domain Overview

**URL**: lukhas.eu
**Status**: Active - Compliance Portal
**Priority**: Critical - Regulatory Compliance
**Role**: EU-specific regulatory and ethical transparency hub

---

## Audience Profile

### Primary Audiences
- **EU Regulators**: Policymakers and oversight bodies
- **Compliance Officers**: Organizations verifying EU compliance
- **EU Users**: European users seeking regional compliance info
- **Data Protection Officers**: GDPR compliance verification
- **EU Media & Researchers**: Those studying AI regulation

### Audience Mindset
- Highly concerned with GDPR and EU AI Act compliance
- Needs legal precision and documentation
- Values transparency and accountability
- Expects regional data residency information
- Seeks proof of regulatory adherence

---

## Role & Positioning

**Unique Value Proposition**: "LUKHAS AI in full compliance with European values and regulations"

**Key Differentiators**:
1. **GDPR Compliant**: Full data protection compliance
2. **EU AI Act Ready**: Alignment with EU AI regulations
3. **Data Sovereignty**: EU data stays in EU
4. **Transparent Operations**: Regular disclosure reports
5. **Ethical Standards**: EU ethical AI guidelines adherence

---

## Tone & Voice

### 3-Layer Tone Distribution
- **Academic/Legal**: 70%
- **User-Friendly**: 25%
- **Poetic**: 5%

**Rationale**: Regulatory content demands precision and clarity with minimal creative flourishes.

## Visual Design & Style

### Color Palette
**Domain Accent**: **EU Blue** `#0EA5E9` - European flag colors, trust

**Supporting**:
- Consciousness Deep `#1A1A2E` - Professional documents
- Awareness Silver `#E8E8F0` - Clean, legible
- Guardian Shield `#E17055` - Compliance badges
- EU Gold Stars `#FFD700` - European symbolism (subtle)

### Visual Motifs
- **Scales of Justice**: Balance, fairness, regulation
- **EU Flag Elements**: Subtle integration (not prominent branding)
- **Document Icons**: Legal compliance, transparency
- **Shield with Stars**: Protection + European identity

4. **Data Residency**: EU data center locations, data sovereignty
5. **Ethical Alignment**: EU ethical AI guidelines adherence
6. **User Rights**: Data access, deletion, portability instructions
7. **Legal Documents**: Terms, privacy policy (EU-specific)

## Homepage Structure

1. **Hero**: "LUKHAS AI: European Compliance & Transparency"

## Content Sections

- `/compliance`: Overview of all EU regulatory adherence
- `/gdpr`: GDPR-specific policies and user rights
- `/ai-act`: EU AI Act alignment documentation
- `/transparency`: Quarterly transparency reports
- `/data-centers`: EU infrastructure and data residency
- `/user-rights`: Exercise rights (access, deletion, portability)
- `/legal`: Terms, privacy policy, DPA (EU versions)
- `/contact`: EU office, DPO email, compliance team

## User Experience Principles

- **Clarity First**: Legal language must be accessible
- **Easy Access**: Rights and policies easy to find
- **Downloadable**: All documents available as PDFs
- **Multilingual**: Available in major EU languages
- **Accessible**: WCAG AAA for legal accessibility

## Cross-Domain Integration

**From lukhas.eu**:
- Main platform → lukhas.ai
- Technical docs → lukhas.dev
- Corporate info → lukhas.com

**To lukhas.eu**:
- Compliance links from all domains for EU users
- Footer links: "EU Compliance"
- Regional auto-redirect based on IP (optional)

## Success Metrics

- **Regulatory Compliance**: 100% (mandatory)
- **Transparency Report Publication**: Quarterly on-time
- **User Rights Requests**: <14 day response time (GDPR requirement)
- **Audit Results**: Pass all third-party audits
- **Zero Violations**: No regulatory fines or violations
