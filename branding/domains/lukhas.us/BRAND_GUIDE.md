## Audience Profile

### Primary Audiences
- **US Regulators**: Federal and state oversight bodies
- **Compliance Officers**: Organizations verifying US compliance
- **US Users**: American users seeking regional compliance info
- **Legal Counsel**: Attorneys evaluating LUKHAS legal compliance
- **Policy Researchers**: Those studying US AI policy

- Concerned with US privacy laws (CCPA, state regulations)
- Needs alignment with NIST AI frameworks
- Values transparency and corporate accountability
- Expects adherence to AI Bill of Rights principles
- Seeks clear legal documentation
