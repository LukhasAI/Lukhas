### Interaction Patterns

**Authentication Flow**:
- Clear progress indicators

- Success confirmation (visual + message)
- Immediate access to dashboard

**Security Actions**:

- Success: "Î›iD created successfully" with particle burst

- Loading: "Authenticating..." with subtle animation (not spinningâ€”biometric pulse)
