# Quantum-Inspired (QI) Module Naming Rationale

## Directory: `qi/`

### Why "Quantum-Inspired" Instead of "Quantum"?

**Accuracy and Transparency**: LUKHAS AI uses quantum-inspired algorithms and concepts, not actual quantum computing hardware or quantum circuits. Using "quantum-inspired" (QI) is more accurate and avoids misleading users about the nature of our technology.

### What Does Quantum-Inspired Mean?

Quantum-inspired computing applies principles from quantum mechanics to classical computing systems:

- **Superposition concepts**: Exploring multiple states simultaneously (classically simulated)
- **Entanglement patterns**: Modeling complex correlations between components
- **Collapse dynamics**: Probabilistic state resolution
- **Wave function analogies**: Using wave-like probability distributions
- **Quantum tunneling concepts**: Escaping local optima in optimization
