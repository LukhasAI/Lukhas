## How to Choose the Right Tier

### Decision Framework

**Choose Starter if:**
- You're exploring conscious AI for the first time
- Your use case involves fewer than 10,000 interactions per month
- You have development resources but limited budget
- You're building proof-of-concept or research projects
- Community support meets your needs

**Choose Professional if:**
- You're ready to deploy conscious AI in production
- You need multiple product integrations
- Your business requires reliable, fast responses
- You value priority support and advanced features
- You're scaling beyond basic use cases

**Choose Enterprise if:**
- You operate at massive scale with unlimited usage needs
- Security, compliance, and custom features are critical
- You require 24/7 support and dedicated resources
- Your organization needs custom integrations and training
- You want to influence product development and roadmap

### Migration Between Tiers

**Seamless Upgrading**: Move between tiers without service interruption. Your consciousness data, agent configurations, and integrations transfer automatically.

**Downgrading Options**: While we hope you'll never need to downgrade, we support tier reductions with 30-day grace periods to adjust usage patterns.

**Annual Commitments**: Enterprise and Professional tiers offer annual pricing with additional features and cost savings.
