## Vulnerabilities Found: 1

### CVE-2025-50181 (GHSA-pq67-6m6q-mj2v)
- **Package**: urllib3 1.26.20
- **Severity**: Medium
- **Fix**: Upgrade to urllib3 >= 2.5.0
- **Description**: urllib3 retries parameter ignored on PoolManager, redirects not disabled as expected
- **Impact**: Applications attempting to mitigate SSRF by disabling redirects at PoolManager level remain vulnerable

## Recommendation
Upgrade urllib3 to version 2.5.0 or later to remediate the redirect handling vulnerability.
